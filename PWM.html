Campaign Activities

🎯
Awareness Campaigns
Educational programs in schools and communities to spread awareness about plastic pollution and its harmful effects on the environment.
🛍️
Jute Bag Distribution
Promoting eco-friendly alternatives by distributing jute bags to reduce single-use plastic dependency in local markets.
🚮
Waste Collection Drives
Organized collection and proper disposal of plastic waste from markets, streets, and public spaces to prevent environmental contamination.
📢
Community Mobilization
Engaging local communities, shopkeepers, and residents to participate actively in the plastic-free movement and adopt sustainable practices.
